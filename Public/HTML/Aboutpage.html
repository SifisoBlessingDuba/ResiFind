About us


Welcome to ResiFind dedicated to addressing student housing challenges at CPUT. ResiFind is the best website for finding student a home to go to when arriving at the Cape Peninsula University of Technology. We are an international project helping student far from home and student which are close to the institution, distance is an excuse for not helping students. We ready to help students with accommodation and bursary to further their studies with harmony. Be financially stable and comfortable with ResiFind. YOUR HOME FOR STUDENT HOUDING AND BUSARIES.


At Cape Peninsula University of Technology (CPUT), every year, countless first-year and returning students face a major struggle finding accommodation on time. For many, especially those coming from distant provinces, this lack of housing creates severe hardships. Without access to essential services like showers, kitchens, beds, and privacy, many students are forced to sleep in uncomfortable, unsafe conditions at the student centre. This crisis not only impacts their well-being but also jeopardizes their academic success as they try to balance starting classes with an unstable living situation.


What’s worse, the process of securing student housing at CPUT is slow and often disorganized. Students are placed into residences at random, making it hard to connect with peers in the same programs or form study groups. Those who arrive late and miss out on early residence allocations fall behind academically, adding to their stress and anxiety.


To add to this, many students face financial exclusion due to lack of financial aid. With tuition fees, residence costs, and everyday living expenses piling up, some students are unable to register, obtain necessary certifications, or continue their studies smoothly. The combination of slow housing placements, financial struggles, and a lack of support creates barriers to education for many students, putting their futures at risk.


OUR MISSION. Our platform aims to bring awareness to these pressing issues, share students' stories, and advocate for more efficient, transparent, and compassionate processes at CPUT. We believe that no student should face homelessness, exclusion, or uncertainty while trying to pursue higher education. Through collaboration, resource-sharing, and community support, we are committed to helping students navigate these difficult times and work towards lasting solutions.


Join us in our effort to make sure that every student can focus on what truly matters their education without having to worry about where they will sleep at night.


How to Apply for Student Residence Eligibility:

Ensure you meet the basic eligibility criteria for applying to student residences. Generally, you must be a registered CPUT student or have an acceptance letter. You may need to submit a separate residence application aside from your academic application. Online Application Process: Step 1: Log in to the official ResiFind website at www.ResiFind.ac.za. Step 2: Navigate to the Student Residence section. Step 3: Complete the residence application form online. Ensure you have your student number and other necessary documentation, such as a copy of your ID or passport. Step 4: Choose your preferred residence based on availability and location. Step 5: Submit the application by the specified deadline (usually in September/October for the following academic year). Late applications may not be considered. Note: The application form might ask you about any special accommodation needs (e.g., medical reasons). Provide accurate details here.


Supporting Documents: If required, upload your supporting documents, such as proof of academic registration, financial information (for funding consideration), or medical certificates if applicable. Application Fee:


There might be a non-refundable application fee. Ensure this is paid to finalize your application. Application Confirmation: Once you’ve submitted your application, you will receive a confirmation email or reference number. Keep this safe for future reference.


How to Track Your Application Status Access the Application Portal:

Visit the CPUT Student Portal and log in with your student number and password. If you have forgotten your login details, use the Forgot Password option to reset your credentials. Tracking Process:


Once logged in, navigate to the Residence Application Status section. Enter your application or reference number (provided when you submitted your application) to view updates. The portal will show you the current status of your application (e.g., "Under Review", "Provisionally Accepted", "Rejected", etc.). Status Updates:


If your application is still under review, check back regularly for updates. You will also receive an official email notification once a final decision has been made. Final Decision:


If accepted, you will be given instructions on how to accept the offer and pay the necessary residence fees or deposit to secure your place. If rejected, explore alternative accommodation options or contact the residence office to inquire about waiting lists or additional support. Contact for Queries:


If you encounter any issues or need clarification regarding your application status, you can contact the CPUT Student Housing Department via email at residences@cput.ac.za or call the Residence Office at the provided number on the website.
